Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply enter your location, choose a restaurant, select your items, and proceed to checkout. You can pay online or with cash on delivery.

Our delivery hours are from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week. Some restaurants may have different operating hours, which will be displayed on their menu page.

Our subscription plans offer benefits like free delivery, discounts, and exclusive access to certain restaurants. You can choose between monthly or annual billing, and you can cancel at any time. Benefits apply immediately after subscribing.

To book catering services, you can use our catering request form on the website or contact our customer service team. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for small events and 7 days for larger events.

If you have any issues with your order, please contact our customer support team immediately. You can use the "Help" section in the app or website, or call our customer service hotline. We aim to resolve all issues promptly.
