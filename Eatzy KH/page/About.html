Our School Project

School Project

Eatzy KH is a school project developed by students from the Department of Computer Science at our university. Our goal was to create a platform that showcases Cambodia's rich culinary heritage while applying the web development skills we've learned.

This project was completed as part of our Web Development course, where we were challenged to create a responsive website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We chose to focus on Cambodian cuisine because we believe food is an essential part of our cultural identity.
